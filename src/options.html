Forget Me Knot!


Remind me when I'm on this site to...

Forget Me Knot! lets you define reminders for when you're on particular websites:

"Whenever I'm on xkcd, remind me to send it to my physicist friend if it's about physics" Try it

You can match "particular websites" based on their page title or their URL, and if you're bold, you can use regular expressions. You can also limit how often you get the reminder, so you're not being pestered about it every time you switch tabs.
